What is VR/360 Photography? How is that different from VR/AR Gaming?

360 Photography most often uses a special camera with multiple lenses to capture a spherical panorama. You need 360 rendering software to view the photography after you've shot it. I used Photoshop to create the example above.

When should I choose 360 Photography over traditional photography?

Anytime you want to capture a full experience. Types of 360 Photography may include:

  • Real Estate Photography
  • Event Photography
  • Performing Arts Photography
  • Landscape Photography
  • Sports Photography
  • And much more!
