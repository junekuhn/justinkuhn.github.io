What is Ambisonic Audio?

When you watch a 360 video, you may notice that you have the ability to rotate the video at will. Most Youtube videos have audio that do not rotate with the video. Ambisonics are a speaker-independent audio rendering technology that allows for real-time spatial audio within 360 videos. Youtube is the only platform that supports this technology today. If a Youtube video has ambisonic audio, it will feel like you're there
