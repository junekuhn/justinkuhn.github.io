What is 360 Photography/Videography? How is that different from VR/AR Gaming?

While it's highly-debated whether all 360 media can be considered VR/AR, 360 Photography/Videography most often uses a special camera with multiple lens to capture an entire scene at once. To be viewed on 2D screens, it must be either in Equirectangular Format or rendered at a specific angle in which the view can rotate the image at will. VR/AR Gaming can include this as a sky, a cinematic intro, or an asset, but these games use 3D Models where the player can move around within a space.

What is Ambisonic Audio?

When you watch a 360 video, you may notice that you have the ability to rotate the video at will. Most Youtube videos have audio that do not rotate with the video. Ambisonics is a speaker-independent audio rendering technology that allows for real-time spatial audio within 360 videos. Youtube currently supports this technology, using the AmbiX Format. If a Youtube video has ambisonic audio then you will be hear a different 'panning' when you rotate the video. It is also a crucial component to immersive experiences.
