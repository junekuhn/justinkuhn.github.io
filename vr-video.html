What is 360 Video? How is that different from VR/AR Gaming?

To make a 360 Video, artists most often use a special camera with multiple lenses to capture an entire scene at once. Both Youtube and Vimeo accept 360 videos. Unlike games, the 360 content is something you watch and interact with from a single position.

When should I choose 360 Video over traditional video?

Anytime you want to capture a full experience. Types of 360 Videography may include:

  • 360 Music Videos
  • Event Videography
  • Performing Arts Videography
  • Virtual Tours
  • Sports Videography
  • And much more!
