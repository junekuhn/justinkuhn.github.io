About Me

I'm an artist local to the Triangle

I graduated from NC State in 2018, and have been living in Raleigh since. I've worked professionally with organizations in the area including the Raleigh Civic Symphony Association, Triangle Artworks, and Arts NC State.

I'm in the scene.

I play in this punk-jazz outfit called Megachrome. Feel free to check out our music on Bandcamp.

In general,

I love to venture into newly possible using technology and workflows that are still in development. I value agency and democracy in technology, and that's one of the reasons I built this website myself using Bootstrap. I believe that new media (whether that's VR/AR, Data Visualization/Sonification, or anything else) can have a huge impact on how to see the world, and give us a pathway to change it.

This website was hand-coded by Justin Kuhn, using Bootstrap 4, HTML5, and CSS
